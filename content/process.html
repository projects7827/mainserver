Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long is the Workshop? What are the timings? Where do the sessions take place?

The 4 day Workshop starts every Monday and is taught in intensive sessions of 1 hour each. The sessions start at 6 PM or 7 PM. The sessions take place on Zoom.

Q: Who will be teaching the Workshop?

The bootcamp is taught by experienced and certified language instructors who have trained thousands of children to start speaking fluent English confidently.

 

Q: Will the Workshop cover all aspects of the English language?

The bootcamp will focus on improving your child’s speaking, listening, reading, writing, and phonetics. Any language cannot be learned in just 4 days but we will enable your child to give a speech in English in a short time even if your child hasn’t spoken in English before.

 

Q: Is the Workshop suitable for children of all ages?

The Workshop is designed for children between 4 to 8 years of age. We teach the kids in 2 batches for effectiveness. One batch is for kids between the age group of 4 to 6, and another batch is for kids between 6 to 8 years of age.

Q: Will my child be able to speak English fluently after the Workshop?

The Workshop is designed to give your child the tools they need to speak English confidently and fluently. However, fluency in a language takes time and practice, and continued study may be needed to achieve and maintain fluency.

Q. How is it different from a pre-recorded class?

Our live classes focus on the human touch, and our coaches will hand-hold your child to deliver their first speech. Learning is best when it’s two-way communication between the student and the teacher. It gives an opportunity to understand how well the child is grasping and tailor it as per their need as well as offer a platform for them to interact.

 

Q. Will it be possible to do the class on a smartphone?

Classes can be easily attended on a smartphone. However, it is recommended that a laptop or a tablet be used for a better experience.
